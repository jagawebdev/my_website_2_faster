QUOTES

One of my dreams is to have a wall full of good books that I have read. I believe book takes you to places you have never been, makes you feel emotions that you have never felt and introduces you to new experiences. As I read a book I like to write down quotes that mean something at that moment. So here are few of my favorite quotes.

-Cicero

"Early to bed, early to rise makes a man healthy, wealthy and wise."

-Benjamin Franklin

"In a world where so many are driven by money, status, and fame, the few with the guts to continue creating, regardless of what everyone else might say about their work are heroes in my book."

-Go big or Go home
Kat Von D

"To understand, than to be understood. To love, than to be loved."

"The best way to predict the future is to invent it."

-Steve Jobs

"Happiness is real when shared."

"It is the possibility of having a dream come true that makes life interesting."

-The Alchemist

"Confidence comes not from knowing you know everything. But from knowing you can handle what comes up."

"Practicing altruism is one of the greatest exchange of energy. Helping others without any expectation and without gain is just as much a gift to yourself as it is to the person you give to."
